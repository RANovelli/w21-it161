Let's WIG OUT!

Put your wig hat on with shades to match, then get on down to Screwdriver Bar for Seattle’s best R&B, Soul, and Garage Rock dance party!!

As always, dance contest after midnight with cash and cool prizes to win!!! Wild R&B, Gritty Soul and Raw Garage Dancers played by four of Seattle’s most seasoned but twisted DJs:

Mike Nipper (Emerald City Soul Club/Name of the Game)
Michael Chrietzberg (Emerald City Soul Club)
Kevin Fitzgerald (Kave-In's Wiggle Room/Get Screwed)
Maxwell Edison (Instant Party/Rave Up)
