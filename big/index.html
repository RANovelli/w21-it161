Welcome

Greetings, and welcome to my BIG website project for the IT161 course at Seattle Central College (SCC). On this site, I have created an array of different web development pages for your perusal.

First, the basics of responsive, two-column layout and a responsive navigation menu, including dropdowns, have been addressed to move easily around the site.

Next, we have a Lightbox 2 image gallery with three clickable images to scroll through, as well as some additional image gallery resource information listed to the right.

Following this, our first dropdown menu, with three research topics (including citations), on Social Media Optimization (SMO), Accessibility, and HTML Forms.

Then, the second dropdown menu, with three Google Tools. First is a responsive, educational YouTube video on Search Engine Optimization (SEO), with three additional SEO resources to click on. Next is an interactive Google Calendar, with a link to a fun event and some info on that alongside it; you will see the calendar in different views (month and agenda), depending on what device you are on. The third menu option is a responsive Google Map with SCC contact info, and a live phone number link should you wish to call the school.

Finally, a live HTML form (as mentioned above) where you can send me comments, concerns, or suggestions; give it a try, I may even respond!!

Moving forward, to continue learning and honing my skills, I have several thoughts in mind. One, I will continue taking courses, reading up on evolving industry trends, and following cutting-edge social media accounts to stay relevant and keep an open mind. I am also a firm believer in networking, so I will build my own network of individuals online (in GitHub, etc) and in person, to stay focused, in the game, and available to opportunities. And lastly, I am intrigued by the idea of coding groups to keep the different languages both sharp and fluid at once.

Thank you for taking the time to read through this, I hope you find some value within my site!
